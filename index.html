About Us

Welcome to Dunder Mifflin paper company.

We are a paper company based out of Scranton, Pennsylvania and the 3rd largest paper supplier in the state. We here at Dunder Mifflin are more than just a company. We are a family. We may be a small paper company but what those big paper companies can't offer you that we can is unparalleled customer service. Any time of day, just pick up your phone and dial our number and our in-house cutomer care executive, Kelly Kapoor will always be available at your service.
   So if you are in need of any kind of paper supplies, go on, give us a call. You might end up making a friend for life.
